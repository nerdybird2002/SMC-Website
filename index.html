Welcome to The Student Mobilization Community!

The Cleveland Institute of Art's Global Liberation organization

The Student Mobilization Community is a Cleveland Institute of Art student organization dedicated to educating campus community members and making active change towards the world they want to see. The overarching theme of SMC will be incorporating global liberation and activist calisthenics into students' everyday lives. We will do this through organizing and partaking in events that strengthen the CIA community and beyond. Activities of the organization will include teach-ins, speakers, collective community action, volunteering, and discussions of not only the world we want to see, but how we make it a reality.
