Our super cool events!

General Body Meetings

Come one, come all to our general body meetings! In these meetings, we discuss club logistics, plan events and fundraiasers, and interact with other students who are passionate about their beliefs.

Zine Making

Craft some zines to spread information about global liberation and organizing objectives. You can make your own or collaborate with the group to release our official bi-monthly SMC zine.

Teach-ins

Meet local community members with strengths in organizing around the Cleveland area. Learn about their work and tactics as we discuss current events, issues, and strategies.
